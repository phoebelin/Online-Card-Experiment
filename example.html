Instructions

We will show you a series of gambles with different cards and different payoffs. Each gamble works as follows: Nine playing cards are shuffled and laid down with their backs facing up. Then the player picks a card and the card's suit determines his payoff. In the example below the player wins $1000 if the card's suit is hearts and $500 if the card's suit is spades. Since all cards are hearts the player is guaranteed to win $1000.

Your task is to imagine you own a ticket to play this gamble once and tell us the smallest amount for which you would be willing to sell it.

Example:
Imagine you own a ticket for playing this gamble once. Please enter the smallest amount for which you would be willing to sell your ticket to someone else.
