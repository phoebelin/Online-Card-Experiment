How to play

You will be shown a series of gambles. For each gamble you will be shown a set of playing cards and the payoffs associated with the two suits: hearts and spades. The cards will be shuffled and laid down with their backs facing up. You will then pick a card and the card's suit determines the payoff.

Now imagine you own a ticket for this gamble. Your task is to estimate how much this ticket is worth.
